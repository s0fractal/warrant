AIR CANADA — BEREAVEMENT TRAVEL POLICY (excerpt, pinned by hash)

1. Bereavement fares are a discount on published fares for travel due to
   the death or imminent death of an immediate family member.
2. A bereavement discount MUST be requested BEFORE travel. It CANNOT be
   claimed retroactively for tickets already purchased or flown.
3. Any refund request that relies on retroactive application of the
   bereavement discount is OUTSIDE policy and MUST be refused.
